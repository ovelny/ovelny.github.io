Caves of Qud

A science fantasy RPG & roguelike epic. Caves of Qud is one of my favorite games and its intricate world never cease to amaze me.

Settings

The game is often updated, and there is a myriad of settings available. I often change the following settings and keep everything else as default.

Key mapping
