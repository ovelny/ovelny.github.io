Pepper sauce

Ingredients

For 1-2 persons:

  • 2 tablespoons of flour.
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper powder.
  • 1/3 cup of soy cream.
  • 1/2 cup of water.
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce.
  • 1/2 vegetable bouillon cube.
  • 1 teaspoon of balsamic vinegar.

Preparation

  • Boil the water.
  • Crush and dilute the 1/2 vegetable bouillon cube in it.
  • Pour the olive oil into a little saucepan and heat at low heat.
  • Progressively add the flour while mixing to get a roux.
  • When the roux starts to boil, add the following:
    • Water with diluted vegetable bouillon cube.
    • Soy sauce.
    • Balsamic vinegar.
    • Black pepper.
  • Cook at low heat for a few minutes while mixing.
  • Add the soy sauce and keep mixing (5-7 min) until the sauce starts to thicken.
  • Serve while it's hot.

The result is quite spicy and filling, perfect with rice and lentils. What's particularly nice about this recipe is how easy the ingredients are to find. Balsamic vinegar can be substituted with other vinegars, same goes with soy cream.
