Food storage

A quick reference guide for storing food.

Fresh herbs

  • Wash the herbs and drain the water.
  • Store them upright in a glass of water.
  • Cover the tops with a zip-lock bag or something similar.
  • Average lifespan is 2-3 weeks, around a week for chervil and chives.

Tomatoes

If never refrigerated:

  • Store them stem-side down, on a plate at room temperature.

If refrigerated:

  • Store them at room temperature until fully ripe.
  • Store them in the refrigerator afterwards.

Potatoes and sweet potatoes

  • Store them in a cool, dry place, with no direct sunlight.

Onions, garlic and shallots

  • Store them in a cool, dry place, with no direct sunlight.

Lime and lemons

  • Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Chilli peppers

  • Store them in the refrigerator.
