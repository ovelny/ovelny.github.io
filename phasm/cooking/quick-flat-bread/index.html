Quick flat bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour.
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • between 1/2 and 3/4 cup of water.
  • between 1/4 and 1/2 tablespoon of salt.

Preparation

  • Mix all the ingredients into a large bowl until it forms a single, firm ball of dough. Add extra flour to reach that result if necessary.
  • Cut the dough into 8 or 9 smaller balls.
  • Heat a pan at medium / high heat without any additional oil (7 out of 9 works nicely with my current setup).
  • Flatten 2-3 balls so they look more or less like cute, lil' pancakes.
  • Place them into the pan.
  • Flip them as soon as the edges puff up and the doughs darken.
  • Store them when both sides are done and cook the rest similarly.

The little breads taste like wasa bread if you know those, except they're softer.

The original recipe is optimistic when it comes to prep time, at least with my setup. It takes me around 25 to 30 minutes to cook the 8-9 balls of dough, but it's still quick enough and satisfying to make.
