ficus ginseng

care

a fig tree, commonly referred to as the beginner's bonsai.

  • likes warm and moist climates
  • make sure it gets a lot of indirect light and avoid direct, bright light
  • should be watered once or twice a week, but can tolerate some over or under watering
  • daily misting to maintain humidity is ideal
  • as a rule of thumb, prune back 2 leaves after 6-8 new ones have grown
  • always leave at least 2-3 leaves on a branch
