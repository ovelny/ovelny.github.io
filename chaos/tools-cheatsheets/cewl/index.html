Cewl

Generate wordlists from sites with cewl.

Commands

# Generate wordlist from site with:
# * Crawling with a depth of 4 on specified site.
# * Words with a minimum length of 6 characters.
# * Lowercase all words found.
# * Store the results in <wordlist>.
cewl <website> -d 4 -m 6 --lowercase -w <wordlist>
